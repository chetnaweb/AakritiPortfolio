Hey Everyone, I'm
Aakriti Narula Entrepreneur

Aakriti Narula is a dynamic businesswoman and entrepreneur, playing a key role in expanding ASHIN’s presence in the fashion industry. She leads ASHIN’s online department and manages the Punjabi Bagh store in Delhi, ensuring a seamless shopping experience for customers both online and offline. Born into a family passionate about fashion, Aakriti is the daughter of Mr. Ashwani Narula, a textile engineer and the visionary behind ASHIN, and Mrs. Madhu Narula, a marketing expert. Growing up in an environment filled with creativity and business expertise, she developed a strong sense of style and a keen eye for trends. With a deep understanding of modern retail and e-commerce, Aakriti is committed to making ASHIN accessible to a wider audience. Her leadership and innovative approach continue to drive the brand’s growth, making stylish and high-quality fashion available to women across India and beyond.
